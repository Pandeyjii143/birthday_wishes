For you, bhaiya

A letter on your 26th

Dear Bhaiya,

Twenty-six looks good on you. I say that half-jokingly, but I mean it — every year seems to add more to who you are instead of just adding a number.

I don't say this enough, so let today be the excuse: thank you for being the kind of brother who shows up. For the calls that start with "just checking in," for taking my side even when I was probably wrong, for every small thing you've done that you probably don't even remember doing.

I hope this year gives you everything you've been quietly working toward, and a few things you didn't even know to ask for. Wherever life takes you next, know that home — and I — will always be right here.

Happy 26th birthday. I love you, always.

— Ravikishan Pandey
